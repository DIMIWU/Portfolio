Soy Mami

Project Overview

Soy Mami is a multi-flavored soy milk from the United States that is popular with teenagers. This design is for three flavors of packaging and website design

The Soy Mami is a soy milk product that comes in three flavors.I found some related pictures to bring inspiration.

According to the flow characteristics of soybean milk, I designed three different Logo.According to the user group of this product is mostly teenagers, scheme 3 is taken out.

According to the three flavors of soy milk, I have designed the patterns of "Unsweet", "Strawberry" and "Banana", so that users can feel the taste of soy milk more intuitively.
